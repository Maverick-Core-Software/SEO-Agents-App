## Style Prompt

Premium cinematic enterprise hardware commercial on a pure black canvas. The visual language is private infrastructure: black anodized metal, graphite glass, electric-blue light moving with controlled precision, and restrained studio lighting. The energy feels alive but governed, circulating inside and around the Maverick Core without implying cloud, internet, people, robots, fake UI, or holograms.

## Colors

- `#000000` absolute black background
- `#05070a` near-black product atmosphere
- `#101820` graphite metal
- `#176bff` controlled electric-blue energy
- `#7fc7ff` hot inner pulse highlights
- `#d7e6f7` rare specular text/highlight

## Typography

- Display and brand: `Archivo Black`
- Technical micro labels, if needed: `IBM Plex Mono`

## Motion

- Slow, confident, premium pacing
- Camera motion uses gentle orbit, macro pan, and restrained focus pulls
- Energy uses finite, closed-loop circulation patterns only
- No chaotic flicker, RGB gaming effects, clouds, globes, robots, people, brains, or fake screens
